About The Platform

Finance Tracker is a comprehensive, client-side personal finance management application. Designed with Outfit typography and smooth CSS micro-animations, it offers multi-workspace user partitions, secure simulated netbanking synchronization for Indian bank ledgers, and interactive graphical analytics.

Your financial data resides safely within your local namespace prefix, ensuring maximum privacy and local storage efficiency.
